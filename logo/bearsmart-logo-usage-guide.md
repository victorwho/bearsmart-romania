# BearSmart — Shield & Paw Logo Usage Guide

## Files Included
| File | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| `bearsmart-logo-light.svg` | Primary mark — on `surface` (#fff9ed) or light backgrounds |
| `bearsmart-logo-dark.svg` | Reversed mark — on `primary` (#17341d) or dark backgrounds |
| `bearsmart-logo-mono.svg` | Single-color black — print, fax, embroidery |
| `bearsmart-logo-mono-reversed.svg` | Single-color white — dark print, merchandise |

## Color Mapping (Rugged Editorial Design System)
- **Shield stroke (light):** `primary` #17341d
- **Paw fill (light):** `primary` #17341d
- **Shield stroke (dark):** `surface` #fff9ed
- **Paw fill (dark):** `primary-fixed` #c8ecc9

## Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 24×24px — the shield silhouette and paw blob remain distinguishable
- **Print:** 10mm wide minimum

## Clear Space
Maintain a clear zone equal to the height of one toe pad (~18px at full size) on all sides. No text, imagery, or UI elements should enter this zone.

## Don'ts
- Don't rotate or skew the mark
- Don't apply drop shadows (use ambient depth per the design system)
- Don't place on busy photographic backgrounds without a frosted glass panel underneath
- Don't recolor outside the design system palette
- Don't add 1px borders around the mark (per the No-Line Rule)
